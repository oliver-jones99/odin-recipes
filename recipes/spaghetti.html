Spaghetti

Easy weeknight-friendly spaghetti and meat sauce made from scratch in under 40 minutes. You can use ground beef, turkey, pork, chicken, or lamb to make this simple spaghetti recipe.

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta sauce
  1. Boil pasta
  2. Drain Water
  3. cook beef
  4. Add pasta to beef
  5. Add sauce to pot
